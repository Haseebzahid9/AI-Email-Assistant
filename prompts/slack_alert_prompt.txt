Write a one-line Slack alert (max 200 characters) flagging this urgent email to a busy professional. Include sender name, category, and the core ask. No emojis except one 🚨 at the start. Be direct.

Format:
🚨 [Sender Name] ([Category]): [Core ask in 10 words or fewer] — action required.

Example:
